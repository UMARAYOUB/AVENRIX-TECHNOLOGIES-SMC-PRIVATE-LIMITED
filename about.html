Our Story

Pioneering Digital Excellence

AVENRIX TECHNOLOGIES (SMC-PRIVATE) LIMITED was founded in 2026 with a bold vision: to build intelligent digital solutions that empower businesses across the globe. Headquartered in Mardan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan, we are a team of passionate engineers, designers, and strategists.

We specialize in software development, artificial intelligence, web and mobile applications, business automation, and cloud infrastructure. Our mission is to deliver world-class technology solutions with precision, transparency, and excellence.

From startups to enterprises, we partner with organizations worldwide to transform their ideas into scalable, high-performance digital products.
